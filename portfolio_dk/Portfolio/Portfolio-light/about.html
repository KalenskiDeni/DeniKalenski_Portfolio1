Get to Know Me

Hi there! I’m Deni Kalenski. I grew up in Bulgaria, was born in Spain, and now live in Denmark. I love traveling to discover new places and meet new people. Besides working in UX/UI Design, I’m passionate about photography and was thrilled to win 1st place in a photography contest in Bulgaria. Taking photos of moments and scenes is something I really enjoy. I am also practising Bulgarian Folk Dances in my free time.


When I’m not exploring or taking pictures, you’ll probably find me out for a run, or in the gym which helps me stay active and clear my mind. I also enjoy hanging out with friends and family, whether it’s going out for a meal, grabbing coffee, or just spending time together. Additionally, I participated in some Erasmus + Projects all around Europe.
